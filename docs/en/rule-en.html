– Rule –

– Rules for school trips

Clothing

Clothing for the activity (including meals at the hotel)

・Wear the standard dress
・Mustn't wear flashy innerwear
・Bags for activities are not specified
・Conform to the rules of school life

Clothing for free time at the hotel

・Inside the room: Jerseys, plain clothes are acceptable (but not revealing clothing)
・Outside the room: Clothes are allowed on the floor where there are no general guests (school-designated jerseys are not allowed)

What to bring

Required

・Bookmarks ・Student notebook ・Watch ・Writing utensils ・Three handkerchiefs (one per day) ・Tissues
・Rain gear ・Change of clothes ・Amenity goods (if necessary)
・Money (transportation, worship fee, lunch, and other expenses necessary for the activity + about 10,000 yen pocket money)
・Masks (4 or more) ・Beverages (more) ・Eco-bag ・Preserved medicine (including medicine for motion sickness)

Any

・Cameras (must be managed at your own risk) ・Snacks (no gum) ・Card games ・Water bottles
・Skin care products ・Alcohol sterilization (if necessary) ・Hair irons

Not allowed

・Game consoles ・Cell phones, smartphones ・Music consoles ・Manga ・Cosmetics, accessories, etc

Activities

How to spend time on the Shinkansen train

・Moving seats or facing each other is prohibited (you may stand and walk only in the restroom)
・Snacks are allowed on board the bullet train, but you must eat in silence

Meals and snacks

・Eat well at the hotel
・Eating and walking are prohibited.
・Snacks may be eaten inside the hotel room and in the bullet train car
・Any kind of snack, but no gum

Shopping other than souvenirs

・Buying drinks from vending machines is allowed
・Convenience stores are available only for "using the restroom," "purchasing drinks," and "purchasing emergency items such as masks"

How to spend time in the hotel

・Going to other rooms during free time is allowed. Going to and from other floors is prohibited
・You may use the TV and refrigerator in your hotel room (TV until bedtime)
・Telephones of the hotel room are not allowed

Prevent the spread of virus

・When eating meals or snacks, do not talk with the mask off
・In hotel rooms, large groups of people do not congregate in one room to avoid density

Other

・Souvenirs should be common sense (buying wooden swords is prohibited, etc)
・Act with good manners and consideration so as not to inconvenience the public

＊ If you have any questions, check with your homeroom teacher.
